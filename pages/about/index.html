About

About Steve Beisner and this site

Work

I'm a writer... of literary and technical works.

I write fiction, poetry, the occasional essay, and I'm one of the editors of the Luna Review, at http://LunaReview.com. I'm a co-founder of Ink Byte Magazine, and Ink Byte Press, which published "writing about writing" for many years and is now on hiatus.

I've also have a special interest in technology as it applies to writing and publishing, and I have taught workshops on various "technology for writers" topics at writers conferences.

I've written software professionally since the mainframe era. My software work includes operating systems and utilities, compilers and interpretors for programming languages, communications software, and applications. I've also managed both software and hardware engineering departments for large corporations and participated in a number of start-ups.

This site, http://stevebeisner.com, is the destination for my "kitchen sink" writing: anything which doesn't fit anywhere else.

In a former life I was a string band musician, playing fiddle, mandolin, guitar, and other strings.

Contacted me to discuss any of the above activities, especially if you've got a project to talk about.

Living Where?

I split my time fairly evenly between New Orleans LA and Santa Barbara CA.
